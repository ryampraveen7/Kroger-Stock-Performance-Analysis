# Kroger-Stock-Performance-Analysis
This project analyzes the recent challenges faced by Kroger, focusing on the implications of its failed acquisition of Albertsons and the subsequent decline in its stock price. It explores the broader economic factors, including inflation and macroeconomic shifts, that have contributed to the company's performance. The research also delves into the financial impact of regulatory hurdles, the ongoing lawsuit, and the company's strategic responses.
